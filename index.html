Wake-Up Flow · 20-25 min · daily

Move with your breath. Slow beats deep. This undoes the desk.

Sprints · Tue / Thu / Sat

5 min brisk walk, two build-ups at 60%, then:

5-6
100 m sprints at 85-90%
Walk back fully between reps. Breathe: hard exhales while sprinting, slow nose breathing in recovery. Wrong if hamstrings feel tight before a rep — skip that session. Never sprint cold.
Sprints keep your heart strong and spike growth hormone without burning the surplus that jogging would. Sunday: flow plus an easy 15-minute walk.

The Week at a Glance

Push-Pull-Legs twice, one athlete day. Beat your logbook in round two. Tap a day to open it.

Every 4th week, cut all sets by one third. Deload weeks are when joints recover and growth catches up. Mark it in your calendar now.

The Rules That Matter

Eight weeks from now you will wish you had started today. You did. Keep showing up.
